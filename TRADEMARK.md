# SearchSteward Trademark Policy

"SearchSteward" and the SearchSteward logo are trademarks of SearchSteward.

Our code is open source under the MIT license; the MIT license covers the
code, not the name. This policy explains what you can do with the name.

## Permitted without asking

- Referring to SearchSteward or its open-source libraries by name
  ("built with SearchSteward's salary-parser").
- Truthfully stating that your project uses, forks, or is compatible with a
  SearchSteward open-source library.
- Linking to searchsteward.com or our repositories.

## Not permitted

- Using "SearchSteward" (or a confusingly similar name) as the name of your
  own product, service, domain, package, or company.
- Using the name or logo in a way that implies SearchSteward endorses,
  maintains, or is the source of your product.
- Using the name on a modified version of our software without clearly
  identifying it as a fork.
